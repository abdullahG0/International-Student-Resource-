Arrival in Scotland

Your first 48 hours — airport, transport, SIM, BRP, police registration.

At the Airport

Show eVisa + CAS

Border Force will scan your passport. Have your university offer letter ready.

Collect Luggage

Edinburgh (EDI) & Glasgow (GLA) — follow signs to Baggage Reclaim.

Airport to City

EDI → Tram (£7.50) or Taxi (£25)
GLA → Bus 500 (£9) or Taxi (£30)
